#VisitMeinCle

Believe it or not, lots of people visit Cleveland. But we need your help to get to 20 million by 2020. Invite your friends, your family or just that one guy from high school by sending them Roast Cards (like postcards, but Clevelandier). Send them digitally or find them at select summer events. Also, invite people via social by tagging them and using #VisitMeinCLE. Because more people in Cleveland means more good times.
